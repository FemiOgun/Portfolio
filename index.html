About me

I'm a self-taught front-end dev based in North London. I started in early 2022 and love combining creativity and problem-solving. Before web dev, my creative pursuits were in music production and I discovered the two fields have a lot in common. I was shocked to discover the similarities between the two regarding being creative, innovative, and problem-solving. Both also require patience, attention to detail, and the ability to iterate and refine ones work blending both my creative and logical sides.


Outside of coding, I keep fit with regular gym, and I also like to play volleyball or basketball whenever I get a chance. I still love producing music and hosting game nights, but most evenings I wind down by binging on anime and sitcoms.


I am actively searching for full-time employment in tech and open to freelance work, I am eager to find a challenging and supportive work environment that will allow me to continue growing as a web developer. My portfolio is just a small taste of what I'm capable of, and I'm confident I can bring unique skills and energy to any team. So, if you're looking for a problem-solving, competitive, and creative front-end web developer, drop me a line!"
